# Password Helper 🔐

An offline-first, privacy-focused tool to quickly generate passwords for password-protected Indian financial and government documents (like Bank Statements, Aadhaar, PAN, etc.).

![Password Helper Interface](https://img.shields.io/badge/Privacy-Offline--First-green?style=for-the-badge)
![Tech Stack](https://img.shields.io/badge/Built%20With-HTML%20%7C%20CSS%20%7C%20JS-blue?style=for-the-badge)

## 🌟 Features

- **Offline-First**: No data ever leaves your browser. All calculations and storage happen locally.
- **Local Profile**: Save your details (Name, DOB, PAN, etc.) once, and get passwords for all your banks instantly.
- **Searchable Database**: Quickly find password formats for various banks and government entities.
- **One-Click Copy**: Generate and copy passwords to your clipboard with a single click.
- **Profile Export**: Save your profile as a JSON file to reuse on other devices or for backup.
- **Modern UI**: A clean, premium dark-themed interface built with Inter typography and Material Icons.

## 📂 Supported Documents

Includes password formats for:
- **Government**: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Income Tax Returns (ITR).
- **Banking**: 
  - SBI (Savings)
  - HDFC (Savings & Credit Card)
  - ICICI Bank
  - Kotak Mahindra Bank (Savings & Neo)
  - Axis Bank
  - PNB (Punjab National Bank)
  - IDBI Bank
  - Bank of India (BOI)
  - Central Bank
  - Yes Bank
  - Standard Chartered
  - Slice / SBM Bank

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

Your privacy is the top priority.
- **Zero Server Interaction**: This app does not have a backend. Your personal details are never uploaded.
- **LocalStorage**: Profile data is saved in your browser's `localStorage`.
- **Open Source**: The logic is transparent and can be audited by anyone.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Clone/Download** this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. Click on **"Create Profile"** in the top right.
4. Fill in your details (only the ones you need for your specific documents).
5. Click **"Save Profile"**.
6. Search for your bank/document and click **"Copy Password"**.
